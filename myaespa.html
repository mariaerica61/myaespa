FANDOM

MY (Hangul: 마이)
/mi/ - collective noun

DEFINITION   :   Represents the devoted fanbase of aespa. Derived from KWANGYA, the realm where aespa's Avatars reside, MY translates to 'The most cherished companion.' The name MY was officially disclosed on November 17, 2020, during an episode of aespa's VLive broadcast.

AESPA

KOREAN: 에스파       JAPANESE: エスパ

“aespa” is a combination of “ae,” which stands for ”Avatar X Experience” and “aspect,” which
means double-sided. Members interact with their digital avatars “ae” and experience new
adventures. Through this unique concept, aespa is creating their own path.

ACHIEVEMENTS

Since their introduction, aespa has received widespread acclaim for their fresh and
inventive take on K-pop. Their musical style encompasses a mix of pop, electronic, and
hip-hop, resulting in a unique yet recognizable sound synonymous with SM's renowned
production quality. With compelling vocals, captivating dance routines, and visually
stunning music videos, the group has amassed a devoted following not only in South Korea
but also on the global stage.

DISCOGRAPHY

AESPA set monumental records with 'Black Mamba' surpassing 21 million views in the first 24 hours of its release, and became the fastest debut MV to surpass 100 million views in the history of K-pop, setting another new record. In May, AESPA released a single 'Next Level', which received enthusiastic response immediately upon release. The MV surpassed 50 million views in just 3 days, breaking self-record. It debuted at No. 1 on Korean and international music charts in addition to reaching No. 3 on Billboard's World Digital Song Sales chart, scoring another worldwide hit.

Following their hit albums 'Girls' and 'MY WORLD', AESPA released an English single 'Better Things'.
